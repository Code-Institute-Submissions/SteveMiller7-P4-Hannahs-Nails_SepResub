The Sanctuary

Set in the heart of Auchterarders High Street, Hannah's Nail Bar has a city meets small town vibe! When opening the salon I knew I wanted to create a fun, welcoming space for all clients. From friendly chat, to the bright & airy surroundings, to the pick & mix... not forgetting the catchiest playlists. I love all things nail art and never shy away from an intricate challenge. Once I'm in the creative zone there are no limits!

Since opening in 2019 I have been lucky enough to have seen a great number of locals and visitors alike. Whatever your circumstances you are always welcome at my little slice of nail heaven. My doors are open Tuesdays to Saturdays so please feel free to get in touch.
See you soon!
